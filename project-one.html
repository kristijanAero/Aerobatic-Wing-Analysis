Overview

This project focuses on designing an aerobatic airplane wing inspired by the Zivko Edge 540, optimized for agility and performance.

Goals

The goal is to design a wing that balances lift, drag, and maneuverability specifically for aerobatic flight conditions.

Wing Structure

The wing utilizes a semi-monocoque structure to maintain strength while minimizing weight.

Materials

Advanced composites and lightweight alloys are chosen to optimize strength-to-weight ratio and durability.

Current Status

Preliminary designs have been completed; now moving to simulation and testing phases.

Next Steps

Refinement of wing profiles and preparation for prototype manufacturing.
